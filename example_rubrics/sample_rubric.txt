Essay Grading Rubric

THESIS: Does the essay contain a clear, identifiable thesis statement that presents the main argument?
- Look for a declarative statement that expresses the central claim
- Usually found in the introduction
- Score: 0-10 points

CITATIONS: Are there proper citations throughout the essay?
- Identify all in-text citations (e.g., Smith, 2019)
- Check for variety and relevance
- Score: 0-10 points

SUPPORTING_EVIDENCE: Does the essay provide supporting evidence for its claims?
- Look for examples, data, or reasoning that backs up arguments
- Evidence should be relevant and sufficient
- Score: 0-10 points

STRUCTURE: Is the essay well-structured with clear introduction, body, and conclusion?
- Introduction should set up the topic
- Body paragraphs should develop ideas
- Conclusion should summarize effectively
- Score: 0-10 points

REFERENCES: Does the essay include a properly formatted references section?
- Check for a references/bibliography section
- Verify formatting consistency
- Score: 0-10 points
